H!

Thanks for visiting!

It's been quite a journey to get to a place where I can create this site.
I've always been interested in computers and what they can do.
How they've made the world and virtually everything in it, accessible and for the most part connected in some way
Totally cool!

The beginning of my professional career was in the commercial printing industry.
I was fortunate enough to have been able to work my way up in the industry.
From deliveries, pre press, finishing and at one point a production manager.
High point for me in this industry was setting up the digital production segment for Aerios Direct.

When this business was unable to continue, I had to make a decision. Continue on in commercial printing or an industry that had a manufacturing segment or blow it up and pursue something that I found fascinating and I could be passionate about!
I was fortunate enough to have an interview with onTargetJobs. I remember during the interview it was mentioned that "you get to build robots". Oh man, I would get to build robots! I had to have this position!
Lucky for me, I was chosen to be the next Feeds Fulfillment Specialist and my new career path started!

onTargetJobs was the perfect place for me to land. I was able to learn and grow!
Feeds Fulfillment Specialist primary duties were to be the "go between" for the business needs and IT.
At first, this didn't include building "bots" (web scrapers) basically because I didn't know how.
My primary IT contact was gracious and patient teaching me how to utilize the software we used to scrape. I was on my way!

onTargetJobs was aquired by DHI Inc. and luckily most of the brands that I supported would still be viable.
DHI was an awesome place to be employed! I continued learning and developing coding skills and was promoted to Senior Feeds Fulfillment Specialist.
Now I was responsible for all the import and export code bases. I still developed some apps to help automate some tasks for the business and "tasks" I had.

In December of 2018 DHI sold Health eCareers to Everyday Health. Shortly after the sale, I was promoted Software Engineer! A goal was realized! I still developed scrapes, integrated inbound feeds, managed and developed outbound feeds but I was much more involved in creating automated tests cases. I really enjoy automating the test cases. It's like developing small little apps to do a specific task. After doing manual testing for regression, yeah, automating that is well worth it! We transitioned from TFS to a Jira/X-Ray solution for the framework of our testing and I helped create the solution.

It's been quite the journey so far and I'm looking forward to continuing it at my next opportunity!
Oh, yeah, I would love to work and live in Hawaii!! 😎
